Privacy Policy

SnakePro includes two versions of the app:

  • SnakePro (Paid Version) – no advertising
  • SnakePro Free – includes advertising

Data Collection

The developer does not collect, store, or share personal data directly.

  • No personal information is collected by the developer
  • No analytics are used

Advertising (SnakePro Free only)

The free version of the app uses Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service provided by Google.

AdMob may collect and process certain data such as:

  • Device information
  • Advertising ID
  • IP address
  • Interaction with ads

This data is used to provide and improve advertisements.

For more information, please see Google's Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Local Storage

Highscores are stored locally on the user's device only.
