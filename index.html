Privacy Policy

SnakePro is available in two versions:

  • SnakePro (Paid Version) – no advertising
  • SnakePro Free – includes advertising

SnakePro (Paid Version)

The paid version of the app does not use advertising or third-party services.

No personal data is collected, stored, or shared.

All data (such as highscores) is stored locally on the user's device only.

SnakePro Free (Ad-Supported Version)

Data Collection

We do not directly collect, store, or share personal data.

However, third-party services used in the app may collect certain information as described below.

Advertising

The free version of the app uses Google AdMob (Google LLC), a third-party advertising service.

AdMob may collect and process data such as:

  • Device information
  • Advertising ID
  • IP address
  • Interaction with advertisements

This data is used to provide, personalize, and improve advertisements.

Users in the European Economic Area (EEA) will be asked for consent before personalized ads are shown, in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

If consent is not given, only non-personalized ads will be displayed.

For more information about how Google processes data:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Local Storage

Highscores and game data are stored locally on the user's device only and are not transmitted to any server.

Legal Basis (GDPR)

Data processing by third-party services is based on:

  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR), where required
  • Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR), for basic advertising functionality

Data Transfers

Data collected by third-party services may be transferred to countries outside the European Union, including the United States.

User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to data processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time
